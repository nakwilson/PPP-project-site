Our Story

Founded in 2011 by veterinarians Catarina Fakename and Dogatha Pet-Person, Paradigm Pet Professionals employs a talented team of pet experts – "Pexperts" – with certifications in pet health and nutrition.

Garnering customer recognition for outstanding support, our Pexperts have 12 years of experience working with pet owners and pet professionals.

Our mission is to offer resources to help you care for your furry, scaly, feathery, and slimy loved ones.

Employee of the Month

Dr. Georgina Greeb, DVM

Dr. Greeb (or Dr. G, as she likes to be called) is a native of Dallas, TX and graduated from the University of Texarkana in 2017 with a doctorate in Veterinary Medicine.

In her spare time, Dr. G fosters chinchillas that have been abandoned at animal shelters. Her herd currently consists of 7 kits.
